Our Story

Welcome to Oishi Matcha, where tradition meets modernity in every cup. We are passionate about bringing you the finest ceremonial-grade matcha sourced directly from the historic tea fields of Uji, Japan.

Our expert tea masters have carefully curated a selection of premium matcha beverages and treats, each crafted with precision and love. From traditional whisked matcha to innovative matcha lattes and desserts, we celebrate the versatility of this superfood.

Step into our serene space and discover the calming ritual of matcha, a centuries-old tradition that promotes mindfulness, health, and wellbeing.
